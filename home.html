ABOUT US

​​​

JMedia strives to be a platform that gives a voice to the voiceless, visibility to the invisible, and hope to the hopeless. Oftentimes, large swaths of the population are left out of traditional media, which is where JMedia plays a critical role. Our focus is on underserved communities like people of color, women, differently-abled, rural, etc. Our goal is to bring their stories and voices to the masses.
